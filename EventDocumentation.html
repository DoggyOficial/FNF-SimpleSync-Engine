Event Documentation

  • SetCamera: Set the camera
  • < Player > Get the player
  • < Opponent > Get the Opponent
  • < Gf > Get the Gf
  • < Custom > Get a custom value

Event examples

  • SetCamera:< Player > Change the camera to the player
  • SetCamera:< Opponent > Change the camera to the Opponent
  • SetCamera:< Gf > Change the camera to the Gf
  • SetCamera:< Custom{X:100}{Y:200} > Change the camera to the custom Position
